About the club

The radio club was revived in 2017 by the help of Ëurekà
members. This humble club revolves around stimulating the tired minds
of engineering students with beautiful tones and melodies during the
breaks between sessions.
We are trying to restore it to its former glory and add more meaningful
activities along the way , and we'll be happy if you stayed tuned with us during
this journey.
